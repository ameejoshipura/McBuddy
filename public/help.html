FAQ

Q: What is the purpose of McBuddy?

To find you study buddies for your McGill Courses!

Q: How does McBuddy work?

Once you join McBuddy, you will automatically receive match recommendations based on other users who are taking the same courses as you. Alternatively, you can also receive and send requests to other users to become their study buddy.

Q: How do I view all pages in French?

You can click on the FRANÇAIS link at the top of any page!

Q: How do I know if I am matched with someone?

A notification will be displayed on your Home page!

Q: How will I be notified of a new incoming message?

A notification indicating the number of unread incoming messages will be displayed next to Messages tab header.

Q: How do I report a user or a message?

You can contact us and we will take care of it! Please send us a screenshot of the inappropriate behaviour that you would like to report.
